Policy

Notice

The information collected by Happy Camper Music Festival includes:

Contact Information - This includes your first and last name, email address, billing and mailing address, and phone numbers provided by you when purchasing products from us, contacting our customer service team or when creating an online account with us.

Profile Data - This includes information such as your username and password you set when creating an account with us. Your age, gender, biographical details, links to your profiles on various third party sites including social networks, interest, preferences, and information about your participation in our promotions, contests or surveys, are all included to the extent at which you consent and disclose.

Communications - This simply includes any information exchanged when between you and our team members, including when you contact us with questions, concerns, feedback or otherwise.

Transactional Payment Information - This is information needed to complete your orders through our website such as name, credit card information, bank account number, billing and shipping information, and your purchase history.

Marketing Data - This includes your preferences for receiving messages about our products, promotions, newsletters, and otherwise, and details about how you engage with our communications.

Use of Cookies: Happy Camper Music Festivals's website, online services, email messages, and advertisements may use "cookies" and other similar technologies to collect information that assists Happy Camper Music Festival to improve products, advertising, customer communications, and the overall shopping experience.

Most browsers let you remove and/or stop accepting cookies from the websites you visit. To do this, follow the instructions in your browser’s settings. Many browsers accept cookies by default until you change your settings. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all functionality of our service and our site may not work properly. For more information about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set on your browser and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org. We use Google Analytics to help us understand user activity and patterns on the site.

You can limit the use of your information for interest-based advertising by:

  • Blocking third party cookies in your browser settings.
  • By using privacy browsers or ad-blocking browser plug-ins that let you block tracking technologies.
  • Using your mobile device settings to limit use of the advertising ID associated with your mobile device for interest-based advertising purposes.
  • If you choose to connect to our service through your social media account such as Facebook, you may be able to use your settings in your account with that platform to limit the information we receive from it. If you revoke our ability to access information from a third party platform, that choice will not apply to information that we have already received from that third party.

Choice

You have the following choices with respect to your personal information:

Opt-Out of Marketing Communications: You may opt out of marketing-related emails and similar messages by using the applicable unsubscribe procedure (for example, by clicking an unsubscribe link in an e-mail or adjusting your website user account preferences), or by contacting us at service@happycamper.com or at 1-555-555-555. From time to time, Happy Camper Music Festival may use your personal information to communicate with you regarding important matters, such as information about your purchases or updates to our Privacy Policy or the terms of use that govern our website. Because that information is important, you may not opt out of receiving these communications.

Use of Third-Parties: Any information you provide or post to third-party social-networking sites and other sites is governed by their own privacy policies, which you may read on the applicable site. You are solely responsible for the information that you choose to post or share in those situations. Happy Camper Music Festival has no control over, or responsibility or liability for, the use, disclosure, and retention of the information that you disclose in those situations, and the use, disclosure, and retention of the disclosed information is not subject to this Policy. Please be careful when disclosing your information in those situations.


Access

You can request the following information about how we have collected and used your personal information on our website:

  • The categories of personal information that we have collected.
  • The categories of sources from which we collected personal information.
  • The business or commercial purpose for collecting and/or selling personal information.
  • The categories of third parties with whom we share personal information.
  • Whether we’ve sold or disclosed your personal information, and if so, the categories of Personal Information received by each category of third party recipient.

You can help us maintain the accuracy and completeness of your information (including your Happy Camper Music Festival website account information) by notifying us of any changes to your information. You may update your Happy Camper Music Festival website account information through our website.


Security

Happy Camper Music Festival retains your personal information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or such longer retention period as may be required or permitted by applicable law.

We will store and process your personal information in Canada, the United States, and other countries. The laws of other countries regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and retention of personal information may be different from the laws of Canada and the United States, and their laws may be different from the laws of your country. The disclosure of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy may result in your personal information being transferred outside of Canada, the United States, or your country.

Happy Camper Music Festival employs reasonable safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the personal information in its possession or under its control in order to protect that information from unauthorized access, collection, use, disclosure, disposal, or similar risks. While Happy Camper Music Festival uses reasonable efforts to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security of your personal information.
